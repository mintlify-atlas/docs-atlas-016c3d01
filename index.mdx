Control de Vacaciones is a web-based HR leave management system built on Laravel and Filament. It gives employees, managers, and HR administrators a single place to submit, review, and approve vacation requests, paid time off, and compensation days — with a full audit trail and email notifications at every step.

## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the admin panel">
    Navigate to `/admin` and sign in with your credentials. Your dashboard shows request statistics relevant to your role.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a request">
    Go to **Solicitudes → Solicitudes de Vacaciones** and click **Nueva Solicitud**. Select your start and end dates — business days are calculated automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track approval progress">
    Your request moves through the approval workflow: **Draft → Pending → Manager Approval → HR Approval → Approved**. You receive an email at each stage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Access to features depends on your role. Employees see only their own requests; managers see their department; administrators have full access to all records.
</Note>
